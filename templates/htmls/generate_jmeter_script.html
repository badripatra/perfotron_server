PerfoTron JMX Generator

How to use PerfoTron JMX Generator :

  • Download Sample Performance Scenario
  • Fill up Details like Thread Group Name, HTTP Request , Method etc. Details on each field mentioned below
  • Upload it and Click Submit. PerfoTron will convert your scenario to Jmeter Script (.jmx) and download to your local system
  • Download Jmeter and test the above Jmeter Script.
  • Once you validation is over use PerfoTron JMX Convertor
